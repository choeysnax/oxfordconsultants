Who We Are



Oxford Consultants is a young, dynamic, bi-lingual, progressive company established to offer business support, strategic advisory and consultancy services with a full spectrum of capabilities in a myriad of areas of business, namely entities registrations, company secretarial services, corporate governance, compliance, immigration, nominee services, liquidation, tax advisory and allied services, translation services (French and English), and human resources services.
